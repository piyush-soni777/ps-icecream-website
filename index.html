OUR STORY

Scooping Happiness Since 1987

Frosty Delights began as a small family-owned ice cream cart in downtown Chicago. Our founder, Maria Rodriguez, started with just three flavors and a dream to bring joy through delicious frozen treats.

Today, we still use Maria's original recipes but have expanded to over 50 locations nationwide. Every batch is still made in small quantities to ensure the highest quality and freshest taste.
